# Business Areas — the big picture

**Why.** This is the higher-level view: the capabilities you just found, grouped into the major parts of
the business — like Sales, Delivery, and Finance. It comes after capabilities because you can only group
things once you've found them. Later, these areas become the modules of the software.

**How.** Click **Generate areas**. The same capability map is now coloured by area, with a legend on top.
Click an area to rename it; on a capability, use the **Area** drop-down to move it to a different area.

> **Example.** Lead Management, Customer & Offer → "Sales & Onboarding"; Design, Procurement, Installation
> → "Delivery"; Billing → "Finance".
